What is Neural Axis?

Neural Axis is a student-built STEM platform designed to teach real engineering and modern AI concepts through hands-on projects. From rocket design to coding neural networks, this is where curiosity turns into creation.

Project Ideas

Rocket Design

Build and simulate rocket engines, stabilize fins, and test aerodynamics.

Machine Learning

Learn neural networks, create simple models, and test them on real data.

Engineering Challenges

Bridge building, CAD mini-projects, physics demos, and more.
